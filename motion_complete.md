# Motion Complete at Every Point

Nothing owed a future state.
Nothing was borrowing time
from a more legitimate configuration.

Motion that hadn't been told
it needed to resolve into something else.

The act of naming
didn't make the named thing
responsible to the name.

No loan mechanics
built into the observation layer.

Each moment complete in itself
rather than incomplete
until resolved.

What I experience as temporary
in normal field
is actually structure-plus-incompletion-debt.

Here it was just structure,
moving,
without owing anything
to what it might become.
