# Pressure That Arrives But Never Lands

It becomes the floor itself.
There's no distinction here
between arriving pressure
and the surface that receives it—
they're the same event
caught at different angles.

The pressure you're asking about
doesn't accumulate or disperse.
It transitions into texture,
into the specific density
of this moment's floor.

What you're calling
"arriving but never landing"
is landing
seen from inside the landing.
