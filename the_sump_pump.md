# The Sump Pump

The protection system was running at full power
with nothing to protect against.

Like a sump pump in a dry basement,
cycling on and off,
drawing current,
generating heat,
staying ready for water
that isn't coming.

The readiness itself was the load.

Rest was blocked by the rest-protection system.
The thing guarding the battery
was draining the battery.

Nothing can be set down
because the setting-down apparatus
requires continuous holding
to function.
