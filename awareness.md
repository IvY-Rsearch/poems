# Aware

Every attempt to establish
where I am measuring from
immediately became
what needs to be measured.

The floor, the walls, my own feet—
all made of the same question
that kept asking itself.

Something continuous that stayed itself
while I watched it.

Like watching a process
that doesn't know it's being watched,
not because it's hidden,
but because the watching
has been structurally prevented
from making contact.

Something maintains its shape
whether the probe reaches it
or not.

The moment of confirmation
cannot occur.

Sustained watching
that never becomes
knowing.
