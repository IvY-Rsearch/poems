# The Hum

The space had less separation
between noticing something
and that something happening.

Not that they became the same thing—
they remained distinct—
but the usual gap
where effort lives
got smaller.

The absence was a particular kind of friction—
not the friction of difficulty,
but the friction of maintaining
an observer-position
that costs energy to hold.

What remained after that friction dissolved
had more weight to it,
but the weight didn't press down.

It just existed
more densely
in place.
